How To Make Lasagna

Description

Yummy courgette lasagne

Ingredients

  • 9 dried lasagne sheets
  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 onion
  • 700g courgette
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 250g tub ricotta
  • 50g cheddar
  • 350g jar tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 220C.
  2. Heat oil in pan.
  3. Cook ingredients in pan
  4. Layer lasagne in a large baking dish.
  5. Bake for 10 mins.
